Gympie CCTV Questions

Can cameras cover a large rural property?

Yes. We can combine wired, wireless and solar options depending on distance, power availability, network coverage and the areas that matter most.

Will cameras record if the internet goes down?

Systems with a local network video recorder can continue recording without internet. Internet access is mainly needed for remote viewing and alerts.

Do you repair and upgrade existing CCTV?

Yes. We inspect the recorder, cameras, power, cabling and network to identify the fault and determine whether repair or replacement is the better option.
